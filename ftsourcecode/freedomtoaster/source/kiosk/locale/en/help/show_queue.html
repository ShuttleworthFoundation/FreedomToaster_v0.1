Toasting Queue

This page shows the queues or lists of items that have been chosen to be toasted. The toaster queues at the bottome contain items that are waiting for a toaster to come free so that they can be toasted.

Clearing the Queues

Under each queue there is a button called 'Clear List'. Clicking this will clear the list above it. Use this if you want to start over with a list, or if you have arrived at the Freedom Toaster and somebody else has selected stuff you don't want. You can also clear the queues by clicking the Skip buttons on the toasters on the left until they stop prompting you to put in blank disks.

Selecting more Items

To add more items to your list, or to shop around while the system is busy toasting something, just click on the Home button at the top left of this screen then find what you want and add it to the queue.
