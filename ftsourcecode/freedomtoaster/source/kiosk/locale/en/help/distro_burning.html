Distro Information Help

This screen shows pros and cons about any given linux distribution or open software or content

Toasting the latest Version

  • The system defaults to the most recent or popular version. Click on the 'Add to Selection' button to use this version (recommended for most users).

Choosing other Versions

  • Sometimes older versions are available, or versions for other hardware such as Apple Mac or other non-Intel architectures
  • Click on 'Other Versions' to get a list of other versions that might be available for this item.
  • Choose the alternative version from the list, then click 'Add to Selection' to put it on your list

Canceling the Selection

If you do not want to add a given item to your list to toast, either click the 'Home' button (top left of the screen) to go to the home page, or the 'Back' (<) button (bottom left) to go to the previous page.

Feel free to add anything to your list if you think you might be interested in it - you will get a chance to clear the items you don't want before sending them to the toasters.
